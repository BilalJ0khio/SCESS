Achievements

So far we have renovated one damaged school, Gave solar fans to another school, distributed stationary to more than 12 hundred students and covering the stationary budget of more then 18 schools every month. We gave books and alphabetical charts to some of the schools as well. our purpose is to reach to such under previlaged remote areas schools where students are lacking basic facilities like boards, copies, pencils slates etc. We are trying our best to generate more donors to cover the expenses and we are also motivated to give stationary to more schools.
